Places to Eat

Manchester is a culinary haven with a diverse range of dining options to suit every palate. In the city centre, you'll find renowned restaurants like The French by Simon Rogan, offering a fine dining experience with a modern twist. For those craving international flavours, the Curry Mile in Rusholme is a hotspot for delicious and authentic South Asian cuisine. The Northern Quarter is a hub for quirky eateries, serving everything from artisanal pizzas to vegan delights. If you're into upscale dining, visit King Street where establishments like Hawksmoor excel in steak perfection. For a more casual atmosphere, Altrincham Market is a foodie's paradise with its variety of vendors offering gourmet street food. Whether you're a gastronomic adventurer or prefer classic comfort food, Manchester's culinary scene is sure to satisfy your cravings.

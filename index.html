Manchester

Manchester is a city and metropolitan borough of Greater Manchester, England. Manchester is known for its tourist attractions. Manchester may be England's third city after London and Birmingham, but this former industrial and commercial giant considers itself a step ahead of its rivals. Manchester may now be better known for its football teams than its manufacturing. Manchester became known as the world's largest marketplace for cotton goods and was dubbed “Cottonopolis” and “Warehouse City” during the Victorian era. The best food is found here in Manchester and people are so friendly that you won’t regret planning your trip to Manchester. There's just so much variety, culture and that warm 'northern' hospitality, that makes this city an incredible stop on your England road trip or when exploring more of the best places in Northern England.
